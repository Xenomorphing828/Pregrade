A capture instrument for your phone. It straightens the card, measures centering to the millimetre, and inspects every corner — so an $18–$100 CGC fee only ever rides on a card that earns it.

  • 01Front — straightened

    Live edge-lock finds the card and auto-fires when it's square, still and sharp. The shot is warped flat before anything is measured.

  • 02Back — centering

    Optional, but CGC reads back-centering too. Same locked capture.

  • 03Four corners — required

    A macro on each corner. No warp here — raw pixels, focus-gated — to read whitening and tip wear honestly.

  • 04Verdict + ROI

    A predicted CGC ceiling, per-pillar confidence, and the submit / keep-raw math at current fees.

Before you start: shoot the card out of its sleeve on a plain surface that contrasts with the border — a light desk for dark/black-bordered cards. Glare and low contrast are what break edge-lock.
